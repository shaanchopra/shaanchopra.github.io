Hello World!

Date: Nov 05, 2022

Hello and welcome to my HTML site. I always wanted to learn a bit about HTML and CSS and look at this site, I am learning. This is the first time I am hosting this kind of site, where I have to hardcode everything. Well, it has been great so far on the coding side. I do have some issues on the domain side. It feels like there is a lot of downtime on the DNS. Well, I officially wanted to welcome you to my site and put out a plan, but looking at the issues I am going to keep this short.

I am still working on the layout of the site and also looking for a solution where formatting is done on its own and I am left with only the content. I am also using a VM for hosting this site. Which is also very different from shared cPanel hosting. Overall I am learning both coding and networking.

Google, W3Schools and StackOverFlow have turned out to be great resources for where this site is now. I do get stuck at a lot of places and these sites have just been great in getting me out of those situations. Now, I want to keep going ahead and build this site at par with my other WordPress blog.

Everything till now has been just words on the site but with this post images will also start playing a role. I want to keep everything very organised as I was reading which said that HTML sites can get very messy if not kept in check. Well, I think it is a bit of the above topic of just saying hello world. I will put out the working details in another post. Here just want to say if you have reached here then keep going and keep visiting.
